about us

Commodity Samachar is a sole proprietorship firm in the business of trade or investments. We as a firm deliver complete assistance to all our customers thereby providing a wide range of facts and data about fluctuating trading scenarios. Picking up stocks and generating handsome profits is not a kid’s game. One should have a comprehensive understanding of the fickle market. Moreover, a smart investor or trader requires a genuine set of acquaintances and skills to grow their stocks. Our firm would make you informed about the current situation of the stock market in the form of charts and regular updates. We provide recommendations for all segments i.e. Equity, Commodity, Currency, Crypto, and Forex. Our continuous trading approach would make you aware of the trading opportunities as and when they arise. We deliver direct and prompt recommendations to our traders and investors via WhatsApp so that without any delay in time you can easily trade with your stockbroker. You may also visit our Facebook and Instagram Page for our regular updates.

Moreover, Ankit Kapoor is a research head of our firm who worked for several well-known financial companies. No doubt, his remarkable experience in the financial market would make you count on him. His depth analysis of the luring financial market would provide comfort while taking the best investment decisions.
